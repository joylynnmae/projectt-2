Joy's Top 5 "No- Skip" Albums

curated by Joy Del Rosario

1. Never Enough

by Daniel Caesar

This is the album I play when I want to feel everything. It has a mix of feel good vibes like "Homiesexual" or if you need a goo crying session there is "Cool"- which is my personal favorite. However, my all time favorite song off the record is "Always". This is such a perfect summer album for when you want to have good vibes or cry your heart out

Listen to "Always" here

2. American Teen

by Khalid

I had first listened to this album in my freshman year of high school. It is one of the albums I ever listened to all the way through because I had just gotten Spotify Premium. In my English class, my friend and I listened to this album during our classwork time. The most fmous track on this record is "Location". However, my favorite track is "Therapy".

Listen to "Therapy" Now.

3. Ctrl

by SZA

This is the album I would play in a traffic jam. Every song on this record is something you can belt out and makes traffic go by fast. My friends and I blast "Supermodel" almost everytime we are in the car together.

Listen to "Ctrl" here

4. Hurry Up Tomorrow

by The Weeknd

The Weeknd is my all time favorite artist. This album has been on repeat for me since it has came out. My favorite track is "Timeless" with Playboi Carti. Hearing this album live and in convert recently made me appreciate this album a little more.

Listen to "Timeless" Now

5. Blonde

by Frank Ocean

Frank Ocean's "Blonde" is a great album if you ever need to cry or let things go. This album has so much soul and feelings. Some of the feelings on this album, you may have never experienced before, but Frank Ocean uses lyrics to make you feel everything he is trying to convey. A top track on this record is "Godspeed" and I highly recommend you to give it a listen.

Listen to "Godspeed" Here
